Rodrigo Porto

Princeton Math '27

Hi! I'm Rodrigo, a junior at Princeton studying Mathematics with minors in Statistics & Machine Learning. I'm broadly interested in large language models for mathematics — improving reasoning, formal theorem proving, and the gap between competition-style math and real research math.

Some notable things worth mentioning:

  • Research at Princeton with Prof. Sanjeev Arora's group on LLM math reasoning and formal theorem proving (see Escaping the Cognitive Well and Goedel-Architect).
  • Founding Member of Technical Staff at Vigil Labs, developing agents to trade crypto.
  • Previously SWE intern at Amazon (global inventory systems) and Hillspire LLC (mobile apps and diffusion-model infrastructure).
